IAT 313 Skill Building Assignment: Stop Motion Animation - The Dance

Genre: Comedy

About

This is a stop motion project from IAT 313. I was in charge of filming and editing the video, and I used Premiere Pro to edit the short film.

Process

At first, we were brainstorming on the concept we wanted to focus on. Since the project’s date is in the month of Halloween, we think that it would be a good idea to film a Halloween related film, therefore, we came up with this film. It is about a skeleton entering an audition to become a superstar. My partner and I made the background and props and we filmed for 3 days. It was a tough time because we had to take a lot of pictures and make them into a video. During the editing process, some of the images are too dark, and I colour graded the images to make the film more lively. I combined the film and looked for the sound effect for the characters. It took me a while to find the sound effects because it is hard to find an effect that is related to a figurine.

Thoughts

Although the film is not long, it teaches me how to deliver the film message to the audience within a short amount of time. Also, it teaches me how to edit still images using different fps.
